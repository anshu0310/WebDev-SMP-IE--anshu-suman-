Sharks of Indian Navy-Easing the Oxygen crunch

Specialised teams of Indian Navy will be sent through all the coastal states of India for boosting the oxygen needs and repairing the oxygen plants,this has been confirmed by chief of Indian Navy Admiral Karambir Singh.The action was taken after seeing the condition of our country in this pandemic and a meeting with PM was organised,there Admiral Karambir Singh declared to help the states with the accesories which were manufactured within Naval Dockyard and human resources too.The Eastern Naval command(ENC) also agreed to fix leakages in oxygen plants , and check the condition of plants.
